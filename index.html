A milk dispenser powered by Arduino is a clever and efficient solution for automated milk dispensing. Arduino, a versatile microcontroller, can be programmed to control the milk dispensing process based on user input or preset measurements. By integrating sensors, such as flow sensors to measure the amount of milk, and actuators like pumps to control the flow, the Arduino can accurately dispense desired quantities of milk. This not only simplifies the process for users but also ensures consistency in portions, making it ideal for applications in cafes, households, or other settings. The Arduino's programmability allows for customization, remote monitoring, and potential integration with other smart systems, enhancing the overall milk dispensing experience.



A smart water tap utilizing Arduino offers an innovative approach to water conservation and convenience. By incorporating sensors and actuators, the Arduino can detect the presence of hands or objects beneath the tap and activate the water flow accordingly. This touchless functionality promotes hygiene and reduces water wastage by controlling the flow duration based on user requirements. Through Arduino programming, the tap can also offer features like temperature control and flow adjustment, enhancing user comfort. The system's adaptability and potential connectivity to other devices make it a sustainable and modern solution for homes, public spaces, and commercial establishments, contributing to both water efficiency and user satisfaction.
